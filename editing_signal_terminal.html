    On scraping, honestly. Reddit (RSS), Bluesky (open API), GitHub and OpenAlex are free and durable — wired into the pipeline. X/Twitter has no sustainable free scrape (login-walled; workarounds get banned) — track those people on Bluesky instead, or log posts by hand. LinkedIn actively blocks and litigates scraping — there's no safe free route, so its signal (hires, team-size, exec moves) comes from press + your own eyes via the log button. That human-in-the-loop step is a feature: it's the judgment a tracker can't automate. Verify figures before sharing.
